Skiing in Slovakia

In Slovakia (Slovensko), you can look forward to 464 kilometres of slopes: the ski resorts are served by 532 ski lifts. Have lots of fun skiing in Slovakia (Slovensko)! In the list of the best ski resorts in Slovakia (Slovensko), the ski resort Jasná Nízke Tatry – Chopok is top with 4.0 out of 5 stars. The largest ski resorts offer up to 45 kilometres of slopes (Jasná Nízke Tatry – Chopok). The highest ski resorts for skiing in Slovakia (Slovensko) extend up to an altitude of 2,190 metres (Tatranská Lomnica). And our tip for the best value for money is the ski resort Meander Skipark – Oravice.
